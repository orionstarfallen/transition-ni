    about TRANSition NI

    The goals of our project here at Transition NI are simple. We wish to offer a platform where the transgender and non-binary community of LGBTQIA+ individuals in Northern Ireland can easily access information and resources.

    Northern Ireland currently has very limited healthcare for individuals wishing to transition both medically and socially. The Gender Identity Clinic (GIC) has closed its waiting lists for new patients, with waiting times growing from months to years. This has a serious impact on both the individuals and the community as a whole, and campaigns for better healthcare go unheard by the local government.

    This site aims to expand the accessability of resources and give real life insights into the lives of transgender and non-binary people living across Northern Ireland. It is our strong belief that access to vital and life-saving medical advice should be easy and free, always.

    Our site is currently in the early stages of development, so we kindly ask you for patience as our platform gets up and running.
